If $y= \dfrac{t+1}{t-2}$ and $x = \dfrac{2t+1}{t-3}$, find $\dfrac{dy}{dx}$ when $t = 1$.
\newline
\[
y = \frac{t+1}{t-2}
\]
\[
x = \frac{2t+1}{t-3}
\]
\[
\frac{dy}{dt} = \frac{-3}{(t-2)^2}
\]
\[
\frac{dx}{dt} = \frac{-7}{(t-3)^2}.
\]
Consequently, when $t=1$, at $(-1/2, -2)$,
\[
\frac{dy}{dx} = \frac{-3(1-3)^2}{-7(1-2)^2} = \frac{12}{7}.
\]
